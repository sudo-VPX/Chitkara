Hyderabadi Biryani üçó

Ingredients:

  • Basmati rice - 2 cups
  • Chicken - 500g
  • Yogurt - 1 cup
  • Biryani masala - 2 tbsp
  • Fried onions, mint, coriander

Steps:

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt & spices for 30 mins.
  2. Cook rice till half done.
  3. Layer rice & chicken; cook on low flame (dum) for 20 mins.
  4. Serve hot with raita.

Veg Hakka Noodles üçú

Ingredients:

  • Noodles - 200g
  • Mixed veggies - 1 cup
  • Soy sauce - 2 tbsp
  • Garlic - 4 cloves
  • Oil, salt & pepper

Steps:

  1. Boil noodles & drain.
  2. Stir-fry garlic & veggies.
  3. Add noodles, soy sauce & toss well.
  4. Serve hot.

Chicken Curry üçõ

Ingredients:

  • Chicken - 500g
  • Onion, tomato, ginger-garlic paste
  • Curry powder - 2 tbsp
  • Oil & salt to taste

Steps:

  1. Fry onions until golden.
  2. Add tomatoes & spices, cook well.
  3. Add chicken, cook until tender.
  4. Garnish with coriander & serve hot.

Stuffed Paratha ü´ì

Ingredients:

  • Wheat flour dough
  • Stuffing (potato or paneer)
  • Ghee for roasting

Steps:

  1. Roll dough, fill stuffing, seal edges.
  2. Roll again gently.
  3. Roast both sides with ghee.
  4. Serve hot with curd or pickle.
